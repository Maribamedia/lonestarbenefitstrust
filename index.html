About Us

A Legacy of Stewardship

Since 1996, the Lone Star Benefits Trust has served as a fiduciary partner to Texas school districts. Our reputation for stability and sophisticated strategy now extends to Oklahoma through our exclusive partnership with the Oklahoma State School Boards Association.

We operate on a simple principle: your healthcare dollars should educate children, not enrich shareholders. As fiduciaries to our public sector clients, we provide the transparency, control, and cost management that fully insured carriers cannot offer.
